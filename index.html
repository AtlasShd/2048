Instruction

Classic game 2048 where the goal is to get a block with 2048 points, but you can continue the game after that to set a record.

The game is controlled in three ways:

  • 1. Arrow buttons (up, down, right, left)
  • 2. Swipe in the direction of movement of the blocks
  • 3. Using the touch on smartphones and tablets

The best 10 records will be displayed in the 'Score table'
